Computer Hoaxes
- threats that don't actually exist
- they seem like they could be real

- can consume lots of resources
- forwarded email messages, printed stuff, wasted time

- often an email, tweet, or facebook post

- some hoaxes will take your money

- a hoax about a virus can take up as much time as an actual virus


De-hoaxing
- it's the internet, believe no one
- cross reference
- hoax-slayer.net
- snopes.com

- spam filters can help
- if it sounds too good to be true, ignore it
