Watering Hole Attacks
- when attackers identify a website thet people in a particular industry will visit and then infects it with a virus
  • - effective, the people have already trust the site

- what if your network was really secure?

- the bad guys can't get in
- not responding to phishing emails
- not opening any email attachments

- have the mountain come to you


Executing the Watering Hole Attack
- determine which website the victim group uses
- educated guess, local businesses
- industry related sites

- infect one of these third party sites
- site vulnerability
- email attachments

- infect all visitors
- you are just looking for specific victims


Watching the Watering Hole
- defense in depth
- layer security measures

- firewalls and IPS
  • - stop the network traffic before things get bad
- antivirus/antimalware signature updates
