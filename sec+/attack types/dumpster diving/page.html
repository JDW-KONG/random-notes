Dumpster Diving

- documents are often thrown into the trash

- when someone goes through your trash hoping to find personally identifiable information
- shred all mail before disposing

- important information is thrown out with the trash
  • - attackers will check garbage for documents containing private info
  • - information found can be used for a different attack
  • - impersonate names, use phone numbers
  • - timing is important
  • - attack just after the end of the month or quarter
  • - based on a routine pickup schedule


Is it legal to dive in a dumpster?
- legal in the US
- if it's in the trash, it's open season
- do not intrude on private property


Protect Your Rubbish
- secure your garbage
- fence and lock
- shred your documents
- try burning the good stuff
