Phishing

- social engineering with a touch of spoofing
- often delivered by spam, IM, etc.
- very remarkable when well done
- attempts to collect personal info and login credentials
- carried out by emailing the victim and asking for personal info while pretending to be someone else
  • - information is often used in identity fraud
  • - emails look like they come from a legitimate body

- don't be fooled, check the URL
- usually there's something not quite right
- spelling, fonts, graphics

- vishing is done over the phone
- fake security checks or bank calls
- phishing over the phone
- may leave a voicemail to try and extort info


- Spear Phishing
- phishing with inside information
- a phishing attack aimed at a specific person or group
- makes the attack more believable
- spear phishing the CEO is known as 'whaling'
