Shoulder Surfing
- when a person stands behind someone using a computer so that they can see sensitive information
- can also happen at ATM machines

- you have access to important info
- many people want to see
- spurred by curiosity, industrial espionage, competitive advantage

- this is surprisingly easy
- airports/flights
- hallway facing monitors
- coffee shops

- surf from afar
- binoculars/telescoprs
- webcam monitoring


Preventing Shoulder Surfing
- control your input
- be aware of your surroundings
- use a privacy filter on your computer screen
- keep away from windows and hallways
